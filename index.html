The Badinagean taxonomy

Vernacular Cloud is an effort to envangelize the philosophical nomenclature of Victor Badinage. Badinage's thesis is that a new systematic philosophy suitable for the current epoch ought to simultaneously walk two paths: One route pursues hermeneutical and conceptual mastery by human subjects; A second route promulgates the nomenclature using the technology of controlled vocabularies. A systematic philosophy so conceived would thus combine the traditional topics of grammar, dialectic, and rhetoric with the semantic technologies of taxonomy, ontology, knowledge graphs, social media, and so-called AI.

Each concept in the Badinagean nomenclature is published in SKOS, JSON-LD, HTML format with this canonical URL structure: https://vernacular.cloud/version/UUID
