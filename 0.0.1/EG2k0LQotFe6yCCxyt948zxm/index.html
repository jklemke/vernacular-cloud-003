concept: situation

concept-key: https://vernacular.cloud/0.0.1/EG2k0LQotFe6yCCxyt948zxm/

Definition

The foundational thing/object/event/entity. The thing-in-itself. The thing-as-it-is. Grammatical and syntactical verbiage that helps to locate a nominate clause within its presuppositional world. The paradigmatic situation is an actuality established metaphysically/metalinguistically using the unsayables time, space, pattern, isolate, aggregate, void, self. Further situations--for example fictional situations--can be established metaphorically.
