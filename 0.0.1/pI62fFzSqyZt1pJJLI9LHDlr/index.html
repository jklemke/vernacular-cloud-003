Definition

**Art** is a diverse range of cultural activity centered around _works_ utilizing creative or imaginative talents, which are expected to evoke a worthwhile experience,1(https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Art#cite_note-1) generally through an expression of emotional power, conceptual ideas, technical proficiency, or beauty.
