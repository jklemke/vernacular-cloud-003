Definition

he key terms for the explication of perception, and the categorization of EGO. The content of consciousness has this structure: EGO interprets reception as an aspect of an external object, from the perspective of a subject.

aspect-object-subject is the structural foundation of the fundamental theorem of EGO: MDNMIW. Reception purports to be an aspect of an object from this subjective vantage.
