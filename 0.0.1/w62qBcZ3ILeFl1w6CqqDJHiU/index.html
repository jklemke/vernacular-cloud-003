concept: unsayable

concept-key: https://vernacular.cloud/0.0.1/w62qBcZ3ILeFl1w6CqqDJHiU/

Definition

The unsayables are the metaphysical suspension in which corroboration transpires. The self detects patterns of isolates, aggregates, and voids in time and space.
