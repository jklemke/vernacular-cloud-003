Definition

The naming of the "recognized as" brings a new item into the world, the parameterized object. Intensionality, content of a notion, conjectured object, parameterized object. Sellars: "Grasp of a concept is always mastery of the use of a word." And the mastery of the use of a word gives birth to the concept. see ipseity-haecceity-quiddity, recognized as a specimen of a species, an isolate of an aggregate, a member of a set, a part of a whole.
